1. Go to https://www.npmjs.com/package/json-server
2. Open your project directory for e.g. 'Backend'
3. Open your terminal
4. Hit command "npm init"
5. do not enter any information just do enter .. enter ... enter
6. hit command in terminal "npm i json-server"
7. Create a db.json file with some data
8. Hit command in terminal "npx json-server --watch db.json"
9. Voilla, your project started to watch database...
